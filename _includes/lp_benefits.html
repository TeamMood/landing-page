How TeamMood improves the workplace

Open up conversations

Transparency is key. Everyone in the team sees the data, and talks about it. As a manager, you take part to those conversations to better understand what is going on.

Uncover hidden issues

Thanks to anonymity, get an honest feedback from your teammates. It's easier for someone to say something through TeamMood instead of going to tell in person.

Get a clear picture of which actions need to be taken

Thanks to your daily survey, discover an issue quickly, and address it without waiting, before the issue gets out of control.

TeamMood at a glance

Easy As Pie

Team members will receive a daily email, asking for their mood of the day. One click and you're done! No need to login, no more than 5 seconds per day.

Anonymous

Do not track individuals, but follow the mood at the team level. All metrics are available to everyone in the team, not only to managers.

Analytics

Quickly get a glimpse on what's going on. Wanna know if things are better or worse? Just check the mood's average variation.

Connected

Our seamless integrations with the tools you already use make participating even simpler.

Agile DNA

Based on the niko-niko practice, each team member tells if she/he is having a good or a bad day. Those insights help your team for continuous improvement.

For Remote Teams

Teammates spread all around the world? We manage timezones, so people get notified at the right time.

Secured

Data are encrypted everywhere. TeamMood is enterprise ready with SAML based SSO.
