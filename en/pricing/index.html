What's an active user?

We only charge for active users, ie users that received at least an email asking for their mood during the mood. You only pay as go. You have the ability to deactivate users in your admin panel.

Can I add or remove users at any time?

Yes! TeamMood is pay-as-you-go, and you can easily add or remove users from your account with a couple of clicks.
