Questions on pricing.

What's an active user?

We only charge for active users, ie users that received at least an email asking for their mood during the month. You have the ability to deactivate users in your admin panel. You only pay as you go.

Can I add or remove users at any time?

Yes! TeamMood is pay-as-you-go, and you can easily add or remove users from your account with a couple of clicks.
