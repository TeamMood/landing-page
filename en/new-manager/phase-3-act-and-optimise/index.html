PHASE 3 - Act and Optimise (Days 61 to 90)

Adjusting processes · Celebrating collective wins · Analysing and demonstrating your impact

Objective: execute and refine your decisions using the data and feedback you have accumulated. You are finally moving to structured action.

Adjusting processes

Use all the feedback you have received to optimise how the team works:

  • Eliminate the bottlenecks you have identified
  • Optimise workflows to make daily work smoother
  • Clarify responsibilities a frequent source of friction
  • Establish better meeting cadences : not too many, not too few
  • Formalise your management rituals : one-on-ones, team check-ins, retrospectives

At this stage, TeamMood can also transform the quality of your team meetings. By accessing the week's anonymous comments before each collective check-in, you arrive knowing exactly what is weighing on the team without spending the first twenty minutes fumbling around to find the real topics. Meetings become shorter, more focused, and more useful.

Celebrating collective wins

Marking the first collective victories is not a nice-to-have it is a strong management act. Celebrating is not about throwing a party. It is about proving that change is positive, that efforts are seen and recognised, and that you are moving in the right direction.

Analysing and demonstrating your impact

At the end of 90 days, you need to be able to tell the story of your impact with data not just impressions. That is often where new managers struggle : they have done good work, but they have no numbers to prove it.

If you have been using TeamMood from the start, you have a complete history of how your team's wellbeing has evolved. You can show your leadership a rising curve, comments that have shifted positively, and clear correlations between your actions and improved morale. That is a 90-day report that speaks for itself.

Your 90-day report should include:

  • The state of your team's health : before, during, and after, backed by data
  • The quick wins implemented and their concrete results
  • The processes optimised and the gains generated
  • Your objectives for the next 90 days
