PHASE 6 - Beyond the 90 days

The first 6 months · The first year · Your lasting management rituals

The 90 days are a starting point, not a finish line.

The first 6 months : consolidate

You have laid the foundations. Now it is time to make them solid:

  • Refine your management style based on what works for your team
  • Deepen your relationships with key stakeholders
  • Launch a first structuring project with your team
  • Start developing your future internal leaders
  • Measure and communicate your results regularly

The first year : build

You are now recognised as the manager of this team:

  • Define a clear vision for the next twelve to eighteen months with your team
  • Develop individual skills in a structured way
  • Build your internal and external network
  • Contribute to cross-functional projects beyond your immediate team
  • Prepare your succession: start developing your future relays now

Your lasting management rituals

Managers who endure install regular rituals that structure time and create predictability for the team:

  • Weekly one-on-one with each team member : thirty minutes
  • Weekly team check-in focused on priorities, not status updates
  • Monthly upward check-in with your N+1
  • Semi-annual 360 feedback
  • Quarterly team retrospective

Wellbeing tracking has no end date. Teams evolve, projects change, people go through difficult periods. The manager who maintains an anonymous feedback channel over time is not the one who watches it is the one who cares. And it is that posture, more than any technique, that builds a lasting reputation.

The success formula : solid organisation + active listening + continuous feedback + visible wins + impact data = onboarding transformed into lasting success.
