Why TeamMood?

Effortless mood tracking to measure team morale

Are your teammates happy or sad? Seems like a simple question, but reality is often different than what you think.

TeamMood gives you a clear answer.

By tracking mood and seeing how it evolves over time, you'll be able to proactively take actions to solve issues that arise.

Foster trust & safety with anonymous feedback

Teammates don’t give honest feedback if they fear it will be used against them.

TeamMood makes all feedback anonymous which paves the way for constructive criticism and feedback in a safe and trusted environment.

Feedback history to plan efficient meetings

It’s hard to remember everything that happens over just a few days, let alone a week or worse, a month.

With TeamMood, you get feedback every day.

Meetings can be properly planned and organized by going over this feedback and leads to better decisions.

Analytics that promote continuous improvement

Visualize team moods at a glance with the Niko-Niko calendar and analytics.

Gain powerful insights into what increases and decreases the mood of your teammates over time and nurture a culture of continuous improvement.

Strong team bonding by sending good vibes

TeamMood is also a place of care.

You and your teammates can: react to feedback, share concerns about a bad mood streak, add GIFs to the discussions, and send kudos to congratulate teammates on a success.

How it works?

1/ Mood check

Each teammate receives a message every day to share their mood.

2/ Team health overview

You can look at the feedback of all your teammates and discover topics to discuss.

3/ Discuss issues & celebrate successes

Everyone can reply to the mood of other teammates.

And with Kudos, you can congratulate teammates on a success and bring good vibes.

4/ Find insights to improve team health

Get a look at the evolution of the average mood, most used words, participation rate, and more.
